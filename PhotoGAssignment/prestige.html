1.The main characters' initials spell "ABRA" (Alfred Borden Robert Angier), as in abracadabra, a common word used by magicians.



2.This was writer, producer, and director Christopher Nolan's lowest-rated movie on Rotten Tomatoes, at 76%. It has since been surpassed by Interstellar (2014), at 71%.


3.It is hinted at in this movie and confirmed in the novel that the twins are named Albert and Frederick Borden, hence "Alfred" Borden.


4.Early on in the movie, Sarah's nephew asks of the bird, "Where's his brother?" Foreshadowing the fact that Borden has a twin brother.


5.The editing includes one hundred forty-six time jump cuts, in which the next shot either flashes back or skips ahead to another time period of the storyline. This averages to almost one timeline jump per minute of movie.
